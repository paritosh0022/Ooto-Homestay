About OOtO Homestay

OOtO Homestay is a warm and welcoming homestay located in Shillong, offering travelers a peaceful and comfortable stay. Designed with artistic charm and a cozy atmosphere, the property provides guests with modern comforts while reflecting the cultural spirit of Northeast India.

Guests can enjoy spacious bedrooms, thoughtful amenities, and a friendly environment that makes them feel at home. The homestay is ideal for travelers who want to experience Shillong's local culture while staying in a comfortable and safe space.
